Our Lodge is known as the Jewel of Inhambane due to its excellent beach location, superior accommodation, and warm hospitality. Boasting twelve, 2-bedroom units, three, 3-bedroom units and five, 5-bedroom units ‚Äì fully equipped with everything you need to have the holiday of a lifetime.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your stay

What is included in the accommodation?

All units come with linen, cutlery, crockery, fridge, deep freeze, kettle, gas stove and gas geyser. All units are equipped with ceiling fans and mosquito nets. Please note that we do not provide towels - all visitors should supply their own.

How do I get to the resort?

The road leading up to the lodge is a dirt road and is accessible by vehicles with a 2√ó4 diff lock configuration or a 4√ó4 vehicle, both with well-deflated tyres. We are located 660km from Mbombela, 970km from Pretoria and 480km North of Maputo.

Where can I buy groceries?

There is no need to bring all your groceries on your holiday. There is a selection of shops to choose from: Loja da Tashi (Massavana), Fernando's (Massavana) and Yum Yum (in the Tofo Area). Be sure to check what the shop might stock before heading over the border.

Is the tap water safe to drink?

When at your Mozambique accommodation, it is best to stick to bottled water unless the lodge explicitly states that they have safe to drink tap water.

What activities are available?

Our lodge offers various Mozambique holiday activities including deep-sea angling, rock and surf fishing, fly-fishing, swimming, snorkeling, kayaking and scuba diving at world-renowned reefs including the famous Manta Reef, Mozambique.
